# Our solar mistakes

When we finally got our first single-family house, we were thrilled to be free from HOAs who are against solar, so we wanted to order our panels "yesterday"—but we wish we had known the following before buying...

| Mistake  | Wasted <br/> money | Wasted <br/> days | Remarks |
|----------|--------------------|-------------------|---------|
| Didn't read this document before getting solar panels ;-) | ~$33k | ~3 years | Really think about your solar goals |
| | | | |
| Didn't have 1 year of electric bills before starting. <br/> **Got too few panels** compared to our annual kWh usage. | $200 / year | | Use your electric bill with the **highest kWh** usage and multiply by 12. |
